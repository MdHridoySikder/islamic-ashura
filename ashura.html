The Significance of Ashura (10th Muharram)

• Ashura falls on the 10th of Muharram in the Islamic calendar.
• On this day in 680 AD, the grandson of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), Imam Hussain (RA), was martyred in the Battle of Karbala along with 72 companions.
• This event is a symbol of the struggle against tyranny and injustice.
• They stood against the tyrant ruler Yazid, choosing truth, justice, and sacrifice over oppression.
• Several other major events also occurred on this day — like Prophet Noah’s (AS) Ark landing safely, and Prophet Musa (AS) being saved from Pharaoh.

Ashura reminds us to stand firmly for truth,
justice, and never bow to injustice.
